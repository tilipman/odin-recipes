Healthy Oatmeal

Description

This vegan oatmeal recipe is the ultimate creamy delicious choice and it comes together in mere minutes!

This is no boring old oatmeal. From the first bite to the last, you've going to love the rich creamy flavor like no other! Easily customizable with whatever toppings you like, and the best family friendly meal.

Ingredients

  • Oats (whole)
  • Coconut milk or other milk with high fat content
  • Cinammon and/or other spices
  • Nut butter - PB
  • Chocolate
  • Fruits - apples, bananas, pear, mangos, or berries
  • Honey or maple syrup

Steps

  1. Add the ingredients into a pot over medium low heat and stir until just thickening. About 2-3 minutes.
  2. Remove from heat and add to a bowl. Use toppings of choice and enjoy!
